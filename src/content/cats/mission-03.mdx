Being deployed in the field against known targets is standard fare for day to day operations. However a periodic covert sweep can lead to discovering vulnerabilities in HQ security, and uncover risks that could otherwise be even more destructive than threats from known actors.

## Establishing a Perimeter

External assessment precedes all interior work. Agents must confirm the outer boundary is secure and establish observation lines at key access points before any room can be cleared. A sweep that begins indoors is a sweep already compromised.

## Structural Inspection

Walls, elevated shelving, and hard-to-reach zones are prime concealment locations for embedded threats. A thorough sweep means getting eyes, and paws, on every surface. If it can be climbed, it must be climbed.

All travel containers, luggage, and personal storage represent potential contraband vectors. Protocol requires an agent to physically inspect each item from the inside. In the cold reality of the third millennia there are no shortcuts, there is only thoroughness.
