Field work is tough. The demands of a deployed operative can shift at a moments notice. No amount of preparation can take the place of hard won experience, and yet agents train vigorously to meet the ever evolving needs of modern tactical operations.

Despite the adversity of field work, a dedicated regimen of exercise ensures agents' mental acuity and physical prowess remain sharp for whatever challenges they may find.

## Priority number one: Stealth

An unaware target is an easy target. Knowing how to blend in to your surroundings can provide a strategic advantage and turn drawn-out skirmish into a quick, silent victory.
