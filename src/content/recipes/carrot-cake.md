This is the carrot cake from Reddit user AshamedTax8008. They called it award winning and they were right. Honestly the cake recipe is dialed in, but "the spice must flow" so I added more + I like molasses so I used dark brown sugar.



They didn't include a frosting but I did here.

## Shopping List

### Cake

- 13 oz AP flour
- 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 3/4 tsp allspice
- 3/4 tsp nutmeg
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 10 oz sugar
- 4 oz dark brown sugar
- 4 large eggs (room temp)
- 7 oz sour cream (room temp)
- 4 oz neutral cooking oil (not olive oil)
- 5 oz butter, browned and cooled slightly
- 13 oz carrots, shredded and drained

### Frosting (my version)

- 12 oz cream cheese (room temp)
- 12 oz mascarpone (room temp)
- 2 cups heavy cream (cold)
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1.5 cups powdered sugar

This makes enough for two 10-inch rounds or a half sheet pan.

## Cake

- Preheat oven to 350F.
- Carrots First
  - Peel carrots
  - Put the shredder attachment on food processor
  - Shred those babies then drain them
- Brown the butter, then let it cool a bit.
- In one bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and spices.
- In another bowl, whisk sugar, brown sugar, eggs, sour cream, oil, and browned butter.
- Add wet ingredients to dry and whisk just until combined.
- Fold in shredded/drained carrots.
- Butter + flour your pans.
- Pour into prepared pans.
- Bake until a tester comes out clean, about 45-60 min depending on pan depth.
- Cool completely before frosting.

> 💡 This batter does better split across 2-3 pans instead of one deep springform.

---

## Frosting

- Whip the cold heavy cream to medium peaks, then set aside.
- Beat room temp cream cheese + mascarpone together until smooth.
- Add powdered sugar and vanilla; mix on low until incorporated.
- Remove beaters.
- Fold whipped cream into the cheese mixture until fully combined.

---

## Finish

- If needed, level cake layers.
- Frost between layers, and the outside.

Chill 15-20 min, and eat if you can even wait that long.
