## Shopping List


### Marinade 
- 15 dried guajillo chiles
- 3 dried chile de arbol
- 4 dried ancho chiles
- 2 chipotle chiles
- 2 tbsp achiote paste
- 1/2 tsp oregano
- 1 bay leaf
- 4 cloves
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 0.5 tbsp black pepper
- 1 small garlic bulb (we need **all** the cloves)
- 1/2 white onion
- 1/4 cup crushed pineapple
- 1/2 cup pineapple juice
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

### Meat
- 4 lbs Pork Butt

---

## Prep

1. Remove stems, seeds, and veins from the dried chiles.
2. Bring a pot of water to boil.
3. Add chiles and bay leaf.
4. Boil until chiles are plump and soft (~10-15 min).
5. Remove chiles and reserve the soaking liquid.
6. Grind cloves + cinnamon in a spice grinder.

---

## Blend

1. Add softened chiles to a blender/food processor.
2. Add all the rest of the ingredients in as well except for the chili liquid.
3. Blend and gradually add the chile soaking liquid until desired consistency. (~ 0.5 - 1.5 cups)

---
